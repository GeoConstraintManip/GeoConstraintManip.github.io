Framework Overview

Given language instruction, our framework is composed of:

  • (1). Task decomposition and flow control generation.
  • (2). Geometric constraints generation for each stage.
  • (3). Translate geometric constraints into cost functions
  • (4). Solve for the trajectory with optimization to minimize the overall costs
  • (5). The robot executes according to predicted gripper's poses and trajectory.
